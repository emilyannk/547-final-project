Bureau of Prisons Organizational Chart

The Federal Bureau of Prisons is managed through a Central Office headquarters and six regional offices. Central Office is organized into Divisions, which in conjunction with the regional offices, provide administrative oversight and support to Bureau facilities and community corrections offices throughout the nation. The individuals in charge of these Divisions and regional offices make up the BOP's Executive Staff.
